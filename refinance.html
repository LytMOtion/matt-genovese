Why Refinance

The flexibility a refinance can give you.

Depending on your goals, a refinance can help you in a few different ways:

01

Lower your monthly payment.

If rates or your situation have improved, a rate-and-term refinance can reduce what you pay each month — and over the life of the loan.

02

Shorten your loan term.

Move from a 30-year to a 15- or 20-year loan to build equity faster and pay less interest overall.

03

Tap your equity.

A cash-out refinance lets you access the equity you've built for renovations, tuition, or a second property.

04

Consolidate higher-interest debt.

Roll higher-interest balances into your mortgage to simplify payments and potentially lower your total interest.

A refinance isn't always the right move — it depends on your rate, your timeline, and your goals. Matt will run the numbers honestly and tell you if it doesn't make sense.
