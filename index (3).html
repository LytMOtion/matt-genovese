Loan Programs

The right loan for every story.

Conventional, jumbo, FHA, VA, or non-QM — Matt and the NAF team work across the full range every week.

Most Common
Conventional

Standard fixed-rate and ARM loans with competitive pricing. Often the best fit for buyers with solid credit and 5–20% down.

Higher Loan Amounts
Jumbo

Financing above conforming loan limits — essential in Santa Barbara, where home prices regularly require jumbo-tier loans.

For Those Who Served
VA

Zero-down, no-PMI loans for active-duty, veterans, and qualifying spouses. Matt is part of NAF's military-focused team.

Low Down Payment
FHA

Government-backed loans with down payments as low as 3.5% and flexible credit. Great for first-time buyers.

Flexible Qualifying
Non-QM

Alternative-documentation loans for self-employed borrowers, real-estate investors, and others who fall outside conventional guidelines — including bank-statement and DSCR programs.

The Process

Four steps. One calm experience.

01 — Discover

Understand the goal.

02 — Pre-Approval
