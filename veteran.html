VA Loan Eligibility

You may be eligible if you meet at least one of these.

VA loans are one of the strongest programs available — and many who served don't realize they qualify:

01

90 consecutive days, wartime.

You served 90 consecutive days of active service during wartime.

02

181 days, peacetime.

You served 181 days of active service during peacetime.

03

6 years in the Guard or Reserves.

You have six years of service in the National Guard or Reserves.

04

Surviving spouse.

You are the spouse of a service member who died in the line of duty or from a service-related disability.

Eligibility is confirmed through your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) — Matt can help you request it and walk you through every step.
