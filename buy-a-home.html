Why Buy

The benefits of owning.

There are a lot of loan options — and a lot of reasons it's worth it. A few of the biggest:

01

Every payment builds equity.

Part of each monthly payment goes toward equity you keep — value that can come back to you when you sell, instead of disappearing into rent.

02

An asset that builds credit.

Because you're investing in an asset and paying on it consistently, homeownership can strengthen your credit profile over time.

03

Real tax advantages.

Mortgage interest, property taxes, and sometimes even closing costs may be tax-deductible. Ask your tax advisor about what applies to you.

04

Options built around you.

From low-down-payment FHA to jumbo financing, a wide range of programs gives you flexibility to plan around your goals.
